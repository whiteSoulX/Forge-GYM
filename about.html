Our story

Strong is a practice.

We started FORGE in 2015 with a simple belief: the best training environment is one where serious coaching and human warmth share the same floor.

That still shapes everything we do. Our coaches teach the fundamentals, our programming respects the long game, and our members bring the energy that makes showing up contagious.

The Forge code

How we show up.

01

Coach the person

Every plan starts with your actual life, your starting point, and the goal that matters to you.

02

Respect the reps

Progress is built in the unglamorous details: clean form, consistent effort, and patient practice.

03

Leave room for joy

Training can be demanding without being punishing. We make space for high fives and good music too.
